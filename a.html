What Is Art?

The world is more than just matter.

Science can tell us how to do things, but only art can tell us why.

At its core, art tries to create material representations of immaterial concepts.

Experiences that can be felt, but not described. Lessons that can be learned, but not taught. Ideas that can be heard, but not spoken.

In this way, art shapes the underlying value structures through which we perceive everything else.

But which direction to shape them?
